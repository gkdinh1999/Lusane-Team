1987

In 1987, LUSANE was founded by SAINT CROUSSANT LOURENT in PARIS, FRANCE. LUSANE started as a somewhat irrelevant fashion house, as all businesses start with a humble beginning...

1991

In 1991, LUSANE was struggling as a newly established fashion firm, and hopes were little... but LUSANE kept to its vision and kept producing...

1997

After years of consistentcy and quality production, LUSANE began to find contractors and partners in business, expanding itself with leading named textile manufacturers...

2003

With the help of quality partners and textile manufacturers, LUSANE had the opportunity to be staged and featured in PARIS SPRING Haute Couture by top fashion models during this time, and the firm began to bloom...

2009

From 2009 to Today and continuing on, LUSANE has been and will be producing proud, quality fashion goods that strives to be expressive and classic.
